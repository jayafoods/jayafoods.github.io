How to Create a Shopping List

  1. Interact with any food item from the menus by hovering over it or selecting it. Then a plus symbol ➕ will appear next to the item.
  2. Add the item to your Shopping List by selecting that ➕ symbol. Repeat this step to add more items as desired.
    • To remove an item from your list, simply select the minus symbol ➖ next to it.
    • To clear all selected items and close the Shopping List, use the 'Clear' button.
  3. Once your Shopping List is ready with your desired items, use the 'Copy' button. This will allow you to paste the list wherever you need it, such as in a WhatsApp message to us.

Tip: The 'Search' function also allows you to copy your search results.
