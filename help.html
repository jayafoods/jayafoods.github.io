How to Create a Shopping List

  1. Hover over or tap on any food item from the menus. A "+" symbol will appear next to the item.
  2. Click or tap on the "+" symbol to add the item to your Shopping List. Repeat this step to add more items as desired.
    • If you want to remove an item from your list, you can click or tap on the "-" symbol next to it.
    • If you want to start a fresh list, click the 'Clear' button. It removes all items.
  3. Once your Shopping List is ready with your desired items, click the 'Copy' button. This will allow you to paste the list wherever you need it, such as in a WhatsApp message to us.

Tip: The 'Search' function also provides a Copy feature for your search results.

Caution: Shopping List and Search Results aren't saved after you leave. Please copy selections before exiting.
